Effects of Junk Food

Health Problems: It can cause obesity, high blood pressure, heart diseases, and diabetes because it contains too much fat, sugar, and salt.

Weak Immune System: Junk food lacks vitamins and minerals, which makes our body weaker and less able to fight diseases.

Low Energy and Tiredness: It gives instant energy, but soon makes us feel tired, lazy, and less active.

Poor Concentration: Regular junk food can affect brain function, making it harder to focus, remember, or learn.

Bad Digestion: It can lead to stomach problems like acidity, constipation, and indigestion.
